Putting screenshots of my notes from No Rules Rules.

  • Have a look at Netflix culture deck here.
  • On no vacation policy- If you're not allocated vacation, you don't fear losing it, and are less likely to take any at all.
  • When Netflix hires someone- They assume you have great judgement and judgement is the solution for almost every ambigious problem. Not process. The flip side is that people are expected to work at super-high level or be quickly shown the door(with a generous severance package)
  • On talent density(Talented people make one another more effective)- After the layoffs, with only the most talented 80 people, we had a smaller amount of talent overall, but the amount of talent per employee was greater. Our talent "density" had increased.
  • If you have a group with few merely adequate performers, that performance is likely to spread, bringing down the performance of the entire organization
